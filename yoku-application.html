Yoku

iOS Application

Yoku is an Online to Offline marketing platform which lets customers get the best cashback/rebates on every transaction they make with Yoku registered merchants in and around your city.

UI/UX Design, Product Design, App Design

previous project

next project

Prime Objective:

Deals are the driving force to attract a customer to any brand. Wouldn’t it be great to earn up to 50% cash back on every transaction you make? Yoku aims on giving merchants a chance line this to create real time deals for their customers to enjoy!

A quick simple process where a customer looks for a deal on Yoku and claims it can be instantly redeemed at the respective store my the merchant. Great way to earn money while shopping!

YOKU - Merchant Application

The Goal is to provide merchants a way to broadcast Coupon deals to their customers even at real time. The process needs to be kept minimal as to not burden the merchants with another task besides their daily business.

Sign Up Process

On Registering to the Yoku Merchant App, the Merchant can view:

Coupons List- Consists of 3 Tabs

ACTIVE DEALS: Consists of deals that are currently running at the moment within the given time frame.
SCHEDULED DEALS: While creating a deal, the merchant has the option to schedule a deal for future use by simply giving a further date range.
EXPIRED DEALS: Coupons that expire go into the Expired deals tab. The merchant has the option to store the expired coupons to use sometime in a future use, to revive the deal again OR delete it.

Create a Coupon-

The Merchant can easily create a coupon in less than a minute and can at the moment broadcast it to the entire Yoku community. Details required to create a coupon: Coupon Name, Coupon Images, Coupon Description, Category, Number of Deals, Deal Start Date, Deal End Date, Max deals per Customer, Claim Expiration

Redeem a Customer Coupon-

Once a customer claims a Coupon on the Yoku Customer App, he/she can present the QR code to the merchant. The Merchant scans the QR code and the deal is immediately applied. Depending on the Customer’s mode of payment the Merchant enters the details and the transaction is processed.

User Profile-

The Merchant can Enter and Update business personal details such as the Profile picture, Cover picture, Name, Description, Email Address(s), Mobile number(s) and Social Media links

Starting the Application-

Login > Home Page > Create a Deal > Publish Sign Up > Enter OTP > Create Profile > Address Set Up > Home Page > Create a Deal > Publish

Redeeming a Customer Coupon-

Click the Redeem a Coupon icon > Allow Yoku to Access Camera > Scan QR Code > Cash / Yoku Money > Enter Bill Amount > Submit
